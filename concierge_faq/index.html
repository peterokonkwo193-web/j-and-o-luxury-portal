Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How long does delivery take?

expand_more
Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days and is complimentary on orders over $500. Express delivery (2–3 business days) is available for $35, and Next Day delivery with white-glove concierge service is available for $65. All orders are fully tracked and insured.

What is your returns policy?

expand_more
We offer a 30-day complimentary returns policy on all full-price items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached. Sale items can be returned within 14 days for store credit. To initiate a return, please contact our concierge team or use the prepaid returns label included in your order.

How do I find my size?

expand_more
Each product page includes a detailed size guide specific to that item. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to our size charts. If you're between sizes, our styling team is happy to help — simply reach out via the contact form or WhatsApp. We also offer complimentary exchanges if the fit isn't quite right.

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are processed through our secure, PCI-DSS compliant payment system with 256-bit SSL encryption. We also offer interest-free installment plans on orders over $500.

Are all products authentic?

expand_more
Absolutely. Every piece in the J & O collection is 100% authentic and sourced directly from the brands and ateliers we partner with. We have a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit goods. Each order includes a certificate of authenticity, and our quality assurance team inspects every item before dispatch.

How do I track my order?

expand_more
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking email with a unique tracking number and a link to monitor your delivery in real-time. You can also track your order by contacting our concierge team with your order number. For Next Day deliveries, we provide live GPS tracking of your white-glove courier.
