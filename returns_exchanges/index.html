Returns & Exchanges

30-day hassle-free returns on all orders. Every return is handled personally by our concierge team.

autorenew

30-Day Returns

Return any unworn, undamaged piece within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange — no questions asked.

local_shipping

Free Return Shipping

We provide complimentary return pickup from Lagos and Abuja. Returns from other Nigerian states are subsidised at a flat ₦5,000 rate. International returns at a flat $20 rate.

payments

Refund in 5–7 Days

Once we receive and inspect your return, your refund is processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

STEP BY STEP

The Return Process

01

Contact Our Concierge

Email returns@jandoluxury.com or use the contact form on our Concierge page with your order number and the reason for your return. Our team responds within 4 business hours.

02

Receive Your Returns Label

Within 24 hours, our concierge team will send a pre-paid, tracked returns label and full packaging instructions directly to your email.

03

Pack & Send

Repackage the item in its original dust bag and box. Attach the provided label and drop it at any designated partner courier point. Keep your tracking receipt.

04

Refund Processed

Once received and quality-checked at our atelier, your full refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days. A confirmation email is sent at every stage.

EXCHANGE POLICY

Prefer a Different Size or Colour?

We are happy to exchange any item for a different size or colourway of the same piece, subject to stock availability. If your preferred size is unavailable, we will issue a full store credit valid for 12 months.

For exchanges for a completely different product, we recommend returning your item for a full refund and placing a new order to ensure the fastest fulfilment time.
