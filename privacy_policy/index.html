1. Introduction

J & O Luxury Boutique ("J & O", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or make a purchase.

By using our website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our site.

This policy complies with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and applicable international data protection standards including GDPR where relevant.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly, as well as data collected automatically when you use our site.

INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE

  • —Full name, email address, delivery address, and phone number (when placing an order or registering)
  • —Payment information (processed securely via our payment provider — card numbers are never stored by us)
  • —Email address (if you subscribe to our editorial journal or newsletter)
  • —Messages and enquiries submitted through our Concierge contact form

AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED DATA

  • —IP address, browser type, and device information
  • —Pages visited, time spent on site, and referring URLs
  • —Shopping bag and wishlist contents (stored via session cookies and our database)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • check_circleTo process, fulfil, and deliver your orders, and to communicate order status and tracking updates
  • check_circleTo send editorial newsletters and new collection previews — only if you have explicitly opted in
  • check_circleTo respond to enquiries submitted through our Concierge service
  • check_circleTo improve our website experience through anonymised usage analytics
  • check_circleTo comply with our legal obligations including fraud prevention and tax reporting

We will never use your data for purposes other than those listed above without your explicit prior consent.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies — small text files stored in your browser — to improve your experience on our site. We use only the following types:

ESSENTIAL

Required to keep your shopping bag and wishlist active. Cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality.

PREFERENCE

Remember your preferences such as size, currency, and newsletter dismissal state.

ANALYTICS

Anonymised traffic analytics (Google Analytics). No personal data is stored. You may opt out at any time.

We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes — ever.

We may share limited data with the following trusted partners solely to fulfil your order or provide our service:

  • —Delivery partners (name and address only) to fulfil and track your shipment
  • —Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) for secure transaction processing under their own privacy frameworks
  • —Supabase (our database provider) for encrypted, NDPA-compliant data storage under a signed Data Processing Agreement

All third-party partners are contractually required to process your data only for the specified purpose and in accordance with applicable data protection law.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely via Supabase's encrypted infrastructure in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023. We implement the following security measures:

  • lockSSL/TLS 256-bit encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • verified_userRow-level database security with strict access controls
  • storageOrder and account data retained for 7 years for legal and tax compliance purposes

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and international standards, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

RIGHT TO ACCESS

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.

RIGHT TO RECTIFICATION

Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

RIGHT TO ERASURE

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements.

RIGHT TO OBJECT

Object to or restrict certain types of processing, including marketing communications.

RIGHT TO PORTABILITY

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format for transfer to another service.

RIGHT TO COMPLAIN

Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) at ndpc.gov.ng.

To exercise any of these rights, please email privacy@jandoluxury.com. We will respond within 30 days.
