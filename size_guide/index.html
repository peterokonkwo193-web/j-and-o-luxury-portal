BEFORE YOU BEGIN

How to Take Your Measurements

straighten

CHEST / BUST

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal and parallel to the ground.

sync_alt

WAIST

Measure around your natural waistline — the narrowest part of your torso, typically above the navel.

width_wide

HIPS

Stand with feet together. Measure around the fullest part of your hips, approximately 20cm below the waist.

height

TIPS

Use a soft tape measure. Measure over light clothing or underwear only. Take measurements in centimetres for accuracy.

Fit Notes

Tailoring & Structured Pieces

Our tailored pieces (blazers, trousers, coats) are cut with a structured silhouette. If between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed, editorial drape.

Knitwear & Jersey

Our knitwear is intentionally oversized. For a fitted look, size down one. All measurements are taken on a flat garment — a 2cm tolerance is standard.

Dresses & Skirts

Fit is based on bust for structured styles and waist for wrap and gathered styles. Refer to individual product pages for specific garment measurements.
